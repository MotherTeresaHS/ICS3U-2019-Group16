How It all started

Back in 2019, our founder, Trinity Armstrong was in secondairy school with the goal of succeeding academically and in other aspects of her life. She needed a personalized way of keeping track of tasks and commitments. OverAchiever was created in order to aid millions of students worldwide to achieve their academic and personal goals through organization. This user friendly website can be accessible anywhere through your smartphone, computer or other devices for free! OverAchiever ultimately replaces the need for a paper planner with a better modern solution.
